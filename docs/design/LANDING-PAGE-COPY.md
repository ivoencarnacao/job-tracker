## Tone Guidelines

- **Voice:** Direct, empathetic, confident. Speak to frustrated job seekers — acknowledge their pain, then offer the solution.
- **Register:** Informal but professional. Use "you/your" (EN) and "tu/teu" (PT-PT) — not "we" or "our product."
- **Length:** Headlines are punchy (under 10 words). Descriptions are 1-2 sentences max. No filler words.
- **No jargon:** Avoid "SaaS", "MVP", "aggregate", "pipeline" without context. Use plain language that a recent graduate understands.
