# Domain Vision Statement

## Purpose

JobTracker exists to transform the chaotic, anxiety-inducing process of job searching into a structured, measurable, and strategic activity.

## The Problem We Solve

Job seekers -- recent graduates, junior professionals, and career changers -- send many applications daily and quickly lose track of where they applied, who responded, which follow-ups are pending, what interviews are scheduled, and what the market actually demands. The result is anxiety, missed opportunities, and wasted effort.

## Our Domain's Value Proposition

JobTracker provides two complementary values:

1. **Organization and Tracking (Core):** A single place to register applications, track their progression through a hiring pipeline, manage follow-ups, record interviews, and evaluate offers. The pipeline model reflects the real-world hiring process including states like "Ghosted" (auto-suggested after 21 days of silence), giving users honest visibility into their job search.

2. **Skills and Market Trends (Differentiator):** By capturing skills from job postings, the system reveals which technologies, frameworks, and competencies the market demands most frequently. This turns passive application data into actionable learning and CV-optimization insights.

## What Makes This Domain Unique

- The pipeline is not a generic kanban board; it models the **actual hiring process** with domain-specific states (HR Interview, Technical Interview, Challenge/Case Study, Final Interview, Offer, Ghosted).
- The combination of **tracking + skills trend analysis** in a single tool is the key differentiator from competitors (spreadsheets, Notion templates, Huntr, etc.).
- The domain supports **non-tech jobs** equally (e.g., retail positions), making it valuable for career changers who track both types of applications.
- Follow-ups are treated as first-class domain concepts with their own lifecycle, not just notes on an application.

## Strategic Focus for MVP

The MVP is a **multi-user application** where each user has private, isolated data. It focuses on delivering immediate value with minimal input friction. The user should be able to register an application in under one minute and see a useful dashboard from the very first entry. No email integrations, no AI-generated content -- just clean organization and honest market insights.
