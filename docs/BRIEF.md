# Job Tracker — Product Brief

## Problem

Job seekers — especially recent graduates, junior developers, and career changers — lose track of their applications, miss follow-ups, and have no visibility into which skills the market actually demands.

## Solution

A web application that centralizes job application tracking with a structured hiring pipeline (from saved to offer/rejected/ghosted), built-in follow-up reminders, interview and offer management, and automated skill trend analysis extracted from job posting descriptions.

## Target Audience

- **Recent graduates** (0–1 years experience) — send many applications daily, need structure and clarity to manage parallel processes for the first time.
- **Junior developers** (1–3 years) — in transition or laid off, juggling multiple interview pipelines simultaneously, need to track feedback and timings.
- **Career changers** (requalification) — coming from non-tech backgrounds, need to understand which technical skills are most requested to focus their learning.

## Differentiator

Most job tracking tools are generic kanban boards or spreadsheets. Job Tracker combines **hiring-pipeline-specific tracking** (with states like HR Interview, Technical Interview, Challenge, Ghosted) with **skills trend analysis** — showing users which technologies appear most frequently across their saved job postings. This turns passive tracking into an active learning and strategy tool.

## Business Model (Suggestion)

**Freemium:**

- **Free tier** — full tracking features with a reasonable application limit (e.g., 50 active applications).
- **Premium tier** — unlimited applications, advanced analytics (temporal trends, category breakdowns, export), priority support.

The MVP launches as entirely free to validate product-market fit before introducing paid features.

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Applications created (first week) | 10+ per user | Validates the core value of easy tracking |
| Weekly active users (WAU) | 2+ sessions/week | Indicates habit formation |
| Status update rate | >50% of applications | Users actively managing their pipeline, not just logging |
| Follow-up feature usage | >30% of users | The reminder system delivers value |
| Insights page visits | >25% of users | The differentiator (skills trends) resonates |
| Known final state rate | >60% of applications | Users track outcomes, not just inputs |
