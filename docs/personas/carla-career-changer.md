# Persona C — Carla, Career Changer

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Age | 42 |
| Experience | 20 years in retail management, recently completed a CS requalification program |
| Background | Transitioning from retail operations to tech. Has applied to both tech and non-tech roles during transition. |
| Situation | Needs to understand tech market requirements while building her first developer portfolio |

## Pain Points

- Doesn't understand which technical skills are most requested (Java? Python? Cloud? Testing?)
- Overwhelmed by job descriptions full of unfamiliar acronyms and frameworks
- Needs to prioritize what to learn with limited time
- Struggles to organize applications across very different types of roles

## Needs

- See which skills appear most frequently across job postings to focus her studies
- Understand skill categories (languages vs. frameworks vs. DevOps vs. databases)
- Simple, no-jargon interface that doesn't assume tech fluency
- Track applications to both tech and non-tech roles in the same pipeline
